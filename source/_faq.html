FAQ
Frequently asked questions
  • How do I contact Customer service?

    First, create an account using our website (coming soon) or by downloading our app onto your phone. Enter your contact information, add your vehicle(s) and a default payment method. Pave will retrieve your balance with the tolling agency, so you can review and pay using many different payment methods. You can even charge your tolls to your cell phone bill!

    We will remit your payment to the tolling agency and clear your balance.

    If you are a tolling agency, contact us for pricing.
